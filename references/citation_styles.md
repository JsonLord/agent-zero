# Citation Styles

There are many different citation styles used in scientific writing. The most common styles include APA, AMA, and Vancouver.

## 1. APA (American Psychological Association)

- Used in the social sciences.
- Uses the author-date system of in-text citation.
- The reference list is in alphabetical order.

## 2. AMA (American Medical Association)

- Used in the medical and biological sciences.
- Uses a numbered system of in-text citation.
- The reference list is in numerical order.

## 3. Vancouver

- Used in the medical and biological sciences.
- Uses a numbered system of in-text citation.
- The reference list is in numerical order.
