# Reporting Guidelines

Reporting guidelines are used to ensure that research is reported in a clear, complete, and transparent manner.

## 1. CONSORT (Consolidated Standards of Reporting Trials)

- Used for reporting randomized controlled trials.
- Provides a checklist of items to include in the report.

## 2. STROBE (Strengthening the Reporting of Observational Studies in Epidemiology)

- Used for reporting observational studies.
- Provides a checklist of items to include in the report.

## 3. PRISMA (Preferred Reporting Items for Systematic Reviews and Meta-Analyses)

- Used for reporting systematic reviews and meta-analyses.
- Provides a checklist of items to include in the report.
